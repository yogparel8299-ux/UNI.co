Common Inquiries

How does UNIC.ai handle data privacy? expand_more
UNIC.ai utilizes an "isolated execution" model where your proprietary data never leaves your VPC. We provide zero-trust connectors that mask PII before it reaches third-party LLM providers.
Can we deploy UNIC.ai on-premise? expand_more
Yes, our Enterprise tier includes a full Kubernetes-ready distribution for air-gapped or localized on-premise deployments.
Which models are supported out of the box? expand_more
We support all major foundation models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral) as well as any custom fine-tuned model hosted via our inference engine.
