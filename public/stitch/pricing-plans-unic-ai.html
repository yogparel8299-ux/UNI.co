Billing Intelligence

How are credits calculated? expand_more

Credits are consumed based on the token usage of LLMs, vector database read/write operations, and swarm orchestration time. Each action has a transparent unit price visible in your Studio dashboard.

Can I roll over unused credits? expand_more

Builder and Company plans allow for a 30-day rollover period for up to 20% of your monthly credit allowance. Enterprise plans feature custom rollover terms.

What happens if I exceed my limit? expand_more

We automatically notify you when you reach 80% and 100% of your usage. You can enable "Auto-Topup" or your workflows will pause to prevent unexpected overage charges.
